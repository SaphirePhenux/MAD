The Story of My Life!!

Hello! My name is Andrew and I am a student here at BYU-I. I am a CIT major and I love working with computers. This webpage that I have created tells a short version of my life. I hope you enjoy it!

I was born in American Fork, Utah! In the year 1990 on Thanksgiving day!! When I was a year old, I moved to Indio, California and lived there for a year before moving back to Utah, specifically the Taylorsville area. I lived there for a few years until my family moved closer to the West Jordan/Kearns area when I was 4 or 5. I lived there until the start of 5th grade, when I moved back to the Taylorsville area. I lived there and went to school in Draper and swam for Kearns and later for Copper Hills while I was living there. Then, at the end of my freshman year of high school, I moved to Indiana where I lived until my mission, completing high school and a year of college before I left.

After my mission to Alabama, I flew out to Utah where my parent had moved to right before the start of my mission. I lived with them in Riverton/South Jordan area until I came up to Idaho to go to school! This is the story of my life! Thanks for reading!!

Cheers!! Andrew
